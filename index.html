Japanese Pitch Accent App!

On this site you can learn how to speak Japanese words as a native speaker does, with correct pitch accent.

Studying correct pitch accent will allow you to lose the "foreign accent" over time and will also greatly improve your listening comprehension.

These flashcards that are shown to you using a spaced-repetition system (If you know the answer, you'll see the card less often, whereas if you mark it wrong, you'll see it again soon). Spaced repetition is a well-studied, proven system for absorbing and retaining new knowledge rapidly.
